# M2 development record

M2 remains in progress. This document records development-only gates and must
not be read as an official PPO-versus-BC test result.

## Pure behavioral cloning gate

The full BC run used 100,000 train and 20,000 validation transitions. It
completed 10,000 updates and selected update 5,500 by the frozen rule of
validation objective completion first and action loss second. The selected
checkpoint has validation loss 0.135374 and checkpoint SHA-256
`7eef23a06ea7177d5090ba90be65f8f2f1a847ecb15d81035c21a7e4567949d4`.

## PPO smoke and validation pilot

The real 2,000-step A100 integration gate passed after an intentional stop at
1,000 environment steps and exact checkpoint resume. It completed six rounds,
performed 32 finite optimizer updates, left no ViZDoom workers or GPU process,
and accessed train cases only.

The first 256-step preflight at learning rate `2.5e-4` correctly triggered the
KL guard after one optimizer update: approximate KL reached 0.080645 against a
limit of 0.03. The BC Actor was already low-entropy, while value gradients also
updated its shared visual-recurrent features. The validation-visible learning
rate was therefore reduced to `5e-5`; the KL limit was not relaxed. A repeated
256-step preflight completed four epochs with maximum approximate KL 0.015084.

The subsequent 20,000-step development pilot completed 316 finite PPO updates,
54 train rounds, and 52 train objective completions. Maximum approximate KL was
0.019896, no KL early stop occurred, and the maximum post-clip gradient norm
was 0.5. A validation-only RandomLegal pilot used three frozen seed pairs with
both learner sides and completed the objective in all six games. This small
sample validates the integration and selected optimizer scale; it is not a
win-rate claim and does not satisfy the frozen M2 evaluation gate.

Machine-readable hashes and counts are stored in
`reports/m2/ppo-pilot-manifest.json`. Full logs and checkpoints remain under
ignored `runs/m2/` paths. The next step is to commit the frozen training config,
then implement and commit the paired M2 evaluator before any official test row
is opened.
