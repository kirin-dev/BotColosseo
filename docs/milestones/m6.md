# Milestone 6: anonymous style-perception study

M6 collection is ready but not yet complete. The engineering gate has produced
six hash-bound validation clips and a ten-assignment anonymous study package.
No recognition result is claimed until the project owner collects ten complete
responses and runs the audited analyzer.

## Curated validation clips

The clips are selected from real validation replays, not generated animations
or test cases. They are explicitly curated showcases rather than random
performance samples.

| Style | Clip | Duration | Visible mechanism |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Aggressive | 1 | 25.0 s | 11 hits, opponent HP reaches 0, death, carrier drop, respawn |
| Aggressive | 2 | 25.0 s | two engagements and 6 hits |
| Defensive | 1 | 31.8 s | full 3-1 win; survives at 5 HP before one visible late death |
| Defensive | 2 | 24.9 s | complete no-death episode, self HP reaches 25, three scores |
| Explorer | 1 | 35.3 s | complete episode with upper, lower, and flank route modes |
| Explorer | 2 | 29.5 s | no-death episode with three route modes and varied corridors |

All six replay records are protocol-clean and record
`test_cases_accessed=false`. The neutral HUD exposes self/opponent health, core
ownership, score, macro action, and causal events to the viewer. Opponent
health and event telemetry are observer-only and are never passed to the
policy.

## Blind package

`artifacts/m6-user-study-v2` contains:

- six opaque MP4 files, two per hidden style;
- ten deterministic counterbalanced assignments;
- Chinese participant instructions that explain the first-person viewpoint,
  task, legal actions, combat, HUD, and rating fields;
- a response template;
- a private answer key and hash-bound manifest.

The package manifest SHA-256 is
`ac7705567efc4f22817e98b8227303e42fa311b0461dece1dd1822cc3f338600`.
Participants receive only the instructions, their assignment order, opaque
clips, and response fields. They do not receive the answer key or style-labeled
curation files.
